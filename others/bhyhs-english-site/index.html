About BHYHS

As a core member of the East China Normal University (ECNU) Basic Education Group and the ECNU Affiliated High School (No. 2 High School of ECNU) Baoshan Basic Education System, Shanghai Baoshan Huayao High School International Division offers A-Level programmes.

Upholding and advancing the educational philosophy of No.2 High School of ECNU, the school is dedicated to cultivating global-minded talents through premium curricula, meticulous services, and an elite cultivation approach.

The International Division holds authorizations and accreditations from several prestigious international education bodies. These include official recognition from Cambridge International Education, Oxford AQA, and Pearson Edexcel, supporting overseas university applications and high-quality programme delivery.
